About Us


​With the current situation surrounding us, it is almost impossible for tourism to resume any time soon and even if it did, tourists would not be able to travel anywhere they like or want to. The goal of this platform is to provide Virtual Tours, allowing people to view and learn about any destintion remotely from the comfort of their homes. This would also help potential tourists to get a demo of the destination that they might want to visit physically in the near future and help them decide accordingly whether they want to visit someplace else or not.

This platform aims at compiling all available resources at hand to make the user feel that the tour is as realistic as it can get. For this we have taken the help of Google Street and 360° view, Wikipedia and VR videos to give a completely immersive experience to the user.
